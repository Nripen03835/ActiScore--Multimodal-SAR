Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IntelliLearn platform and its features.

General Questions

What is IntelliLearn?

IntelliLearn is an AI-powered educational platform that provides advanced tools for content analysis, summarization, and learning assistance.

How do I get started?

Simply create an account, log in, and explore our various AI modules including video summarization, document analysis, and research tools.

Is IntelliLearn free to use?

We offer both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic features, while premium plans provide access to advanced AI capabilities.

Technical Questions

What file formats are supported?

We support various formats including PDF, TXT, DOCX for documents; MP4, AVI, MOV for videos; and WAV, MP3, FLAC for audio files.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 16MB for optimal processing performance.

How accurate are the AI summaries?

Our AI models are trained on extensive datasets and provide highly accurate summaries. However, we always recommend reviewing the results for critical applications.

Support Questions

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the Contact Us page. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What should I do if I encounter an error?

If you encounter any errors, please contact our support team with details about the issue, including any error messages and the steps that led to the problem.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions. Please use the contact form to share your ideas with our development team.

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.
